Welcome to HealthHub

HealthHub is an innovative web-based platform designed to empower users with insights into their health and well-being. Users can effortlessly upload and visualize their health-related data, including heart rate, sleep patterns, and exercise logs. Leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, HealthHub provides real-time predictions and valuable insights to help individuals make informed decisions about their health. With a user-friendly interface and seamless integration with AWS services, HealthHub is a comprehensive solution for health data analysis, promoting a healthier and more informed lifestyle.
